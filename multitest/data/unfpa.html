United Nations Population Fund

The United Nations Population Fund publishes its budgets and financials and statistical and financial review as PDF files. These documents can be found within sessions of the executive board section. It also publishes its annual report.

Although the UNFPA has a transparency portal which shows data visualisations on programme expenses, donor contributions and needs, the data cannot be downloaded in full as machine readable data from this site.

Grants/Projects/IATI

The United Nations Population Fund first published IATI data in 2013. It contains data from 2012 onwards with project budgets, commitments and expenditures on a monthly basis. These data are re-used in UNFPA's transparency portal.

Procurement

UNFPA publishes procurement plans and contract awards on its website. The data is published as xlsx files. These are also published on the site of the United Nations Global Marketplace (UNMG) but not as open data.
