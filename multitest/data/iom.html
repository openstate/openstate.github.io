International Organization for Migration

The International Organization for Migration publishes information on its programmes and budget and its annual reports only in PDF format. Although it publishes data on missing migrants it does not publish data on its budget and spending as open data. It does provide a searchable list of projects funded through its IOM Development Fund it does not yet publish IATI data.

Grants/Projects/IATI

The International Organization for Migration joined IATI in 2017 and has committed to start publishing information on its acticvities and results under IATI within a year from joining.

Procurement

The International Organization for Migration only publishes procurement opportunities on its website (not an open dataset) but does not publish information on awarded contracts.
