World Food Programme

The World Food Programme publishes a strategic plan and annual report in PDF but it does not include budget information. It also publishes audited annual accounts as PDF and it has contributions by governments per year online. In addition, via mpdata.wfp.org management information on programmes can be found.

Grants/Projects/IATI

In June 2013 World Food Programme became member of IATI. It publishes data on project activities. In 2015 WFP started to publish data by region.

Procurement

The World Food Programme publishes annual reports on its procurement in PDF. These reports provide only limited information. No information can be found on suppliers. On the site of the United Nations Global Marketplace (UNMG) WFP publishes procurement notices but not contract awards. These data are not published as open data.
