Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO)

FAO publishes its budgets and audited financial statements online but not as open data.

    Procurement

    FAO publishes bidding calender and summary statistics in PDF files on its procurement activities. Contract awards and notices are also published on the site of the United Nations Global Marketplace (UNMG) but not as open data.
